Variant A — Pick-denominated tilt bar

Approach One "tug-of-war" value bar whose full scale is ± a Mid 1st. The needle sits at the exact imbalance, so its position is a pick reading. Big "SIDE A WINS" headline + a margin pill denominated in the nearest single pick.

Primary Who wins (headline) & by how much in picks (bar needle + "≈ a Mid 2nd" pill). Secondary raw consensus totals + value ratio, demoted below a rule.

Reuses gap.firsts for needle position, gap.pick_equivalent.label for the pill — both already in /api/trade/evaluate. Pill tinted with the tier-ladder color of that pick round (2nd = sky).

Tradeoff The bar is the strongest "how much" signal but adds a new visual the demo/in-league verdicts don't share yet. Needs a sensible clamp when the gap exceeds one first (bar pins to the end, pill falls back to "≈ 1.4 firsts").
