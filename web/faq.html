Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you wanted to know about Fantasy Trade Finder.

What is Fantasy Trade Finder?

Fantasy Trade Finder is a dynasty fantasy football tool that compares how you and your leaguemates value every player, then surfaces trades where both sides come out ahead. You build your rankings, we cross-reference with everyone else's, and mutually-attractive trades bubble up automatically.

How does the ELO ranking system work?

Every ranking decision you make — whether swiping trios, reordering manually, or assigning tiers — is decomposed into head-to-head "winner beats loser" comparisons. Those results feed an ELO rating so each player's value updates relative to the rest of the pool. The more comparisons, the more confident the rating.

Default K-factor for a trio swipe is 32. Trade-card swipes use smaller K-factors (8 for "interested", 4 for "pass") so a single trade view can't swing a rating as much as a direct ranking.

What's the difference between 1QB PPR and SF TEP?

1QB PPR is the classic dynasty format — one starting quarterback, one point per reception.

SF TEP is Superflex with Tight End Premium — you can start a second QB in a flex spot, and tight ends score extra points per catch. QBs and TEs are dramatically more valuable in this format.

Your two rank sets are completely independent, so you can hold different opinions about the same player in each format.

Why two rank sets?

Because the same player is worth very different things in different scoring systems. Tier overrides, ELOs, and progress are all tracked per format. When you switch formats the UI shows your ratings for that format only.

How do I sync my Sleeper league?

Enter your Sleeper username on the login screen. We fetch your leagues via the public Sleeper API — no OAuth or password required. Pick a league from the list and we'll import your roster and every leaguemate's roster automatically.

One honest caveat: entering a username doesn't prove you own it. Until you verify with your Sleeper login, anyone who knows your username could open a session that sees this board — verifying locks it to you.

My league is private — what happens?

Sleeper's public API exposes roster data for every league, public or private, as long as you know the league ID. The app looks up leagues for the username entered at sign-in, so it only surfaces leagues that account is actually a member of — but note that username entry alone isn't authentication (see the sync question above about verifying). Private leagues show up exactly like public ones.

Do you share my data?

No — and your raw ranking list is never shown to leaguemates. The tool compares valuations server-side: your rankings influence which trade suggestions your leaguemates see, if you "like" a trade the other manager may see an interest indicator on the mirrored suggestion, and a mutual like reveals that matched trade to both of you. The League page also shows an aggregate "contrarian score" next to your username — how far your rankings deviate from the league consensus overall, not which players you're high or low on. We don't sell or share data with third parties. See the privacy summary at the bottom of this page.

Can I use values from Keep Trade Cut / Dynasty Daddy?

Your personal rank set starts from a consensus seed derived from market sources, so on day one your values look roughly like KTC or Dynasty Daddy. As you swipe, reorder, and assign tiers, your rankings diverge based on your actual opinions. The tool trusts you over the crowd — that's what surfaces trades nobody else sees.

What's a "tier"?

A tier is a group of players you consider roughly interchangeable, labeled by what they're worth in draft-pick terms — worth 4+ 1sts, worth 2 1sts, worth a 1st, and so on down to FA. Assigning tiers is faster than ranking each player individually and locks in a clean ELO band for every bucket. You can override any individual player's value after setting tiers.

What's the I AM SPEED button?

Turn it on and ranking goes turbo: tap your top two players in a trio and the third is auto-ranked last, then the ranking submits and a fresh trio loads immediately. No Confirm click. Great for blowing through the first hundred comparisons.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes — Fantasy Trade Finder has a native mobile app, and the web app is mobile-first and works great in any phone browser. You can also add the site to your home screen for an app-like experience.

Privacy policy summary

We store your Sleeper username, display name, avatar, league rosters, and ranking history so the tool can function across sessions. Your raw ranking list is never displayed to other users; it influences the trade suggestions your leaguemates see, and a mutual "like" reveals the matched trade to both sides. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties outside of that use. Full details, including deletion and export, are in the Privacy Policy.
