Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Fantasy Trade Finder ("we", "our", or "the Service") operates fantasy-trade-finder.onrender.com and the DTF Dynasty Trade Finder mobile app. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and what we don't do with it.

1. Information we collect

  • Sleeper account info. When you sign in with your Sleeper username, we fetch your public Sleeper profile, your fantasy leagues for the current NFL season, the rosters of those leagues, and the display names of your leaguemates. We only read data that is already publicly accessible via the Sleeper API.
  • Your rankings + swipe history. Each trio ranking, tier assignment, trade decision, and reorder you make inside the Service is stored so your rankings persist between sessions.
  • Device token (mobile only). If you grant push notification permission in the app, we store an opaque device token so we can send you alerts when a trade match is created.
  • Usage data. We log API requests for debugging and basic rate-limiting. Logs are retained for up to 30 days.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your Sleeper password. Sleeper's API is public; no password is ever required.
  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any hardware sensors.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or marketing vendors.

3. How we use your data

  • To power the core features: generate trade suggestions, display your rankings, match you with leaguemates.
  • To compute aggregate community values across all users (anonymized — individual rankings are not exposed to other users).
  • To send push notifications when a mutual trade match is created (only if you opted in).
  • To debug and maintain service quality.

4. Who can see your data

Your personal rankings are private — other users see only their own rankings plus our aggregate community consensus (built from all users together, with no per-user attribution). League activity, trades, and match status ARE shared with leaguemates in the same league so trade matching can work.

5. Data retention + deletion

Your account data persists as long as you use the Service. To delete your data, email us at support@fantasy-trade-finder.onrender.com and we'll remove your user record, rankings, swipes, and any device tokens within 7 days.

6. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

We'll post updates here and change the "Last updated" date. Material changes will also be announced in the app.

8. Contact

Questions? Email support@fantasy-trade-finder.onrender.com.
