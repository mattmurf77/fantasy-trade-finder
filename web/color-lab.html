Color Lab — Trios screen

Five accent systems on the same Rank/Trios screen. Type, layout, and construction are identical; only the palette tokens change. Position colors (QB/RB/WR/TE) and tier colors are cross-client invariants and stay fixed in every option — the accents are chosen not to collide with them. Primary drives the CTA, selected card, ticks, and wordmark; secondary drives data highlights (Elo deltas, streak pill, progress count).
